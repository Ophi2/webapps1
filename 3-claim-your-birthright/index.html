BLACK MIDIS

By: Oliver Peterson

WHAT IS A BLACK MIDI?

A black MIDI (invented by Conlon Nancarrow) is an unplayable (usually remix) of a song. It was invented because Conlon Nancarrow made such complex compositions, that no pianist could play it. So he purchased a player piano, and used a hole-punching device of some sort to make his music get played by the pia no. The reason it's called a BLACK MIDI is because if you look at sheet music for the song, there are so many notes that it looks completely black. Nowadays people like Gingeas or BGH Music use a program called Synthesia or Piano From Above to run a digitalized form of BLACK MIDIs.
